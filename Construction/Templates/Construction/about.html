Who We Are

Mororithas Construction is an eccentric and generically customer orientated construction and engineering service provider. The company provides a comprehensive portfolio of skills and services which range in versatility from the building and civil engineering industries to the construction industry as a whole, in South Africa. Functioning in both the private and public business sectors, the company prides itself in delivering the best of services in terms of the quality and effective efficiency of work.

We work with clients to innovate and adapt to their changing needs and wants in a way that is beneficial to both parties in a sense that the best money for value solutions are implemented and creatively constructed.

Established in 2003, from that time we have been offering and providing a diversified range of services to our clients making certain we deliver first-class services on times in correspondence with the budget without compromising the quality of work, the safety of everyone and the environment at large.
